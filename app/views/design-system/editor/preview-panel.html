Panel layout

  • The panel is styled to be visually distinct with a green outline, shadow, and sticky positioning to stay visible when scrolling.
  • It's split into two main sections: a "Previews" header at the top and tabbed content below.

Tabs navigation

There are two tabs:

  • Page preview: Displays a preview of the current question being edited and includes a link to open the preview in a new tab.
  • Error messages: Lists the error messages related to the question.

Skip links

Both tabs include "skip links" for accessibility, allowing users to jump directly to edit sections related to the content, such as editing the page or correcting error messages.

To view the skip link, click inside this example above and press tab.
